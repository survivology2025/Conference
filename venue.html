Venue & Directions

Survivology will take place across two campuses in Kolkata:

19–20 December 2025 — Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIMC), Joka

Address

Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIM Calcutta)
Diamond Harbour Road (near Joka), Kolkata — Postal details and contacts available on the institute website.

Nearest metro & route

Nearest metro station: Joka Metro Station (Purple Line) — approx. 2–4 km from IIMC. Autos and taxis are available from the station. Public buses also connect from Behala, Taratala, and Tollygunge.

Map

Quick routes

  • From Howrah Railway Station: Taxi or cab via Vidyasagar Setu → Diamond Harbour Road (approx 1–1.5 hrs).
  • From Kolkata Airport: Taxi via EM Bypass → Taratala → Diamond Harbour Road (60–90 mins).

21 December 2025 — Jadavpur University (Main Campus)

Address

Jadavpur University — Main Campus
188 Raja S. C. Mallick Road, Kolkata 700032, West Bengal, India.

Nearest metro & route

Nearest Metro Stations: Rabindra Sarobar (Lake) and Tollygunge. From either station, take an auto-rickshaw or taxi to Jadavpur University (10–20 minutes).

Map

Quick routes

  • From Sealdah Railway Station: Taxi or metro to Tollygunge → auto to Jadavpur (30–45 mins).
  • From Howrah: Taxi directly, or take a train to Sealdah then proceed via metro/auto.
